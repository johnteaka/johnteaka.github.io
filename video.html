These videos had been filmed whilst working with not-for-profit organisation Te Ora Hou, where John had to film these video's by himself with nothing more than one SLR camera in a limited time frame. Most of these videos were based off of ideas that he had come up with, whether they were parodies, formal interview-style or light hearted comedic pieces to entertain the audience, which most of the time were young people of the community.

One Six

If you follow the user that owns this video you will be linked back to local reggae band, One Six. John had the pleasure of being an integral part of this 7-piece band, which gave him the opportunity to make video's for an older audience with a directed culture. These video's were all shot with one SLR camera, with some of video's having had the audio edited post filming.
