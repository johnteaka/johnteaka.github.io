Websites/Apps in development

Over the last year, John passed his Diploma in Web Development at Yoobee School of Design. During this time, he was able to start building projects that will later be released to the public. Here are a few:

Experience Up

Experience Up is an application that is being built for young people who struggle to find employment and work experience. With the help of employers seeking volunteers, this app will enable young people to find opportunities in their area easily, log their work as they go to help them build towards a strong CV.

Cemeteries NZ

Have you ever found yourself walking around a cemetery for a long time trying to locate a loved ones grave that you haven't visited in a while? This app will solve this problem. Cemeteries NZ looks to help kiwis to find loved ones gravesites all over the country. People will be able to connect with family they've never met before and add urupa and gravesites that aren't on record anywhere.

Gaming NZ/AUS

In partnership with Monsterbattlesgaming, this app is being built as a tool for the gaming community of NZ and Australia. Users will be able to manage their teams and join local competitions like never before. It will feature a high powered premium service that help team leaders monitor patterns in their teams gameplay to boost their strategies.
